How to Play Imperial Black

🎯 Objective

Be the first player to discard all your cards!

💎 How to Play

  • Match the suit or rank of the top card
  • Click cards to select them (yellow glow), then click "Play Selected"
  • Play runs - multiple cards of the same rank (any suits)
  • Play suit sequences - K♥ → Q♥ → J♥ or 9♠ → 10♠ → J♠
  • Click "Last Card!" when you have one card left
  • Click "Clear Selection" to deselect cards
  • If you can't play, click "Draw Card"

💥 Power Cards

Ace (A): Wild card - can be played on any card
2: Next player MUST pick up 2 cards (can be stacked!)
Black Jack (J♠/J♣): Next player MUST pick up 5 cards (can be stacked!)
8: Must be covered with same suit or pick up 1 card
King/Queen: Must be covered with same suit or pick up 1 card

🛡️ Power Card Protection

Runs protect power cards! If you play a Black Jack or 2 as part of a run, it's covered - no penalty!

⚠️ Last Card Rule

Must declare "Last Card!" when you have one card left, or pick up 2 penalty cards!

🔥 Penalty Stacking

Stack penalties! Play a 2 on a 2 to make opponent pick up 4 cards! Stack Black Jacks for 10 cards!
